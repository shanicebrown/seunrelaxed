And Sew It Was...

Missy IsaMoore is a selftaught designer born in Kansas City, Missouri, raised in Houston, Texas, and lived in Chicago. She started sewing at the age of 13, by altering her school uniforms to standout in her class. Missy then, decided her high school junior year to make her prom dress, and has been drafting and designing her own garments, as well as for friends and family, since. Missy started her official fashion line in 2012, making a name for herself by doing local shows in the Kansas City metro and the Midwest.
